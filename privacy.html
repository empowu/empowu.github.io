Privacy Policy

Updated 2 January 2023

TLDR;

We will only use your data to keep you informed and allow you to use our products.
We won't sell your data or allow third parties to use your data for anything that isn't directly related to our business.

Data we collect and how we use it

When signing up to our "Get notified" mailing list, we collect the following information:

  • Email address - Used to contact you via email
  • IP Address - Used to prevent spam
  • Browser User-agent - Used to prevent spam

Who we share your data with

We currently store or use your data with the following service companies:

  • Zoho Corporation Pvt. Ltd
  • The Constant Company, LLC
  • Google LLC

We use these service companies to perform our business operations but do not sell your data to them nor consent to them using your data outside what is needed to perform our business operations.

Data breaches

We strive to protect your data and prevent unintended access by complying with industry standards and limiting access to your data when it is not needed. If a data breach were to occur we will inform you via email and where possible inform you if your data and what data was affected.

Right to be forgotten

We support your right to be forgotten.

As long as it is legal for us to do so, you can request your data be deleted by emailing us at privacy@empowu.com and listing what data you wish to be deleted.
Proof of identity may be required in cases where it is unclear if the requesting party owns the data. This is to protect against bad actors affecting your service with our products.
